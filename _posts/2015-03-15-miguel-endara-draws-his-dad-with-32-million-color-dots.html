Within 210 hours Miguel Endara finished the picture of his dad and called it Hero. He did it with just one micron pen. It count 3,2 million dots.

Miguel Endara about himself:

Some call me an interactive designer or just enthusiast. But to be honest, I don't even know what I am. I just think that I'm someone who is thinking non stop. Well the good knew is that I'm hired as a Web-Developer. Now that I know what I am I can go and tell it everybody; Hello, my name is Miguel Endara and I'm a Web-Developer.

That technique, to draw just with dots with different distance to each other, is called Pointillism. It was invented by french painters Georges Seurat and Paul Signac in 1886.
