Here we had to design beer bottles. As I was raised in Belgium it might be obvious that I’m fond of beers. I already tried to brew my own beer so I was pleased about this task. I decided to design a bottle for my own beer which I named Peridot (a green gemstone) because of the unique light green gleam the beer has. Enjoy the taste ;-)
