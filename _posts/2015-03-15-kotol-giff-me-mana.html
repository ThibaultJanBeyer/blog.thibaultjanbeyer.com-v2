KOTOL is some weird way a Russian abbreviated Keeper Of The Light (KOTL). KOTL being a Hero from the popular game Defense Of The Ancients (short: DOTA). The community of Hatton Games taped a guy with a strange Russian accent playing KOTOL in a very bizarre way. The video went viral on Youtube (>1,5 Million views). That video made my day, so I decided to design this cool shirt which is selling well.

You can get your own kotol giff me mana shirt and many more on my t-shirt store if you're cool enough.
