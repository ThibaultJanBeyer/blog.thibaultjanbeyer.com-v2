Back in 2011 The two 3D street artists Max and Joe painted the world largest and the world longest 3D street art. Which brought them 2 world records. The artwork has a space of 1160m2 and is 106m long. They painted it within a week at the West Indiana Quay (map) in London. Reebok CrossFit sponsored the event.
